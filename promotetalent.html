Building a digital presence for a professional dancer

UX design

Visual design

Ali Paige Block is a freelance professional ballet dancer based in New York City. She usually lands gigs through networking and word of mouth, and she needed a website to give potential choreographers an overview of her work and experience. She also needed a way for people to contact her easily. I designed a simple, mobile-responsive site for Ali to network and advance her career.
