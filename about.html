about Dribbbble thoughts

Hi! My name is Janey and I am UX/UI designer based in New York. I most recently worked on UX, design research, and product strategy at Studio Subu, a design and communications studio in Mumbai. I've led and worked on print and digital projects for some of India's leading NGO's, including Teach For India, Cipla Foundation, SNEHA, and the Akanksha Foundation.


My expertise lies in user experience design and storytelling to help organizations reach their goals, whether it be behavior change, fundraising, excellent digital experiences, or effective communications. I believe empathy, inclusivity, and good design can play an instrumental role in delivering better outcomes.


I was previously a fellow with the University of Chicago Harris School of Public Policy’s International Innovation Corps. I introduced user-centered design to a project with a major Indian foundation and the city of Mumbai to improve sanitation and waste management in the city.


I hold a Bachelor’s degree in political science from the University of Chicago.
