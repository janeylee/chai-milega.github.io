Hi! My name is Janey and I currently work on research and product strategy at Studio Subu, a design and communications studio in Mumbai.

My interests are primarily in design for behavior change and inclusion in contexts riddled with complexity. Whether this comes in the form of policy development or product and service design, I believe empathy, inclusivity, and good design can play an instrumental role delivering better outcomes.

I was previously a fellow with the University of Chicago Harris School of Public Policy’s International Innovation Corps. I introduced user-centered design to a project with the Tata Trusts and Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai to improve sanitation and waste management in the city.

Even before that, I analyzed a bunch of data for the US Consumer Financial Protection Bureau. It was there I realized that financial services and financial inclusion are areas ripe for user-centered thinking and thoughtful product design. (Check out their design manual ; it's an excellent example of design for effective communication and public services!)

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in political science from the University of Chicago, where I took many excellent courses in policy, law, anthropology, and statistics.
