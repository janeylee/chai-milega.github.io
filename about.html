Hi! My name is Janey and I currently work on design research, content, and product strategy at Studio Subu, a design and communications studio in Mumbai. I've led and worked on print and digital projects for some of India's leading NGO's, including Teach For India, Cipla Foundation, SNEHA, and the Akanksha Foundation.


My interests lie in design and storytelling to help organizations reach their goals, whether it be behavior change, fundraising, excellent user experiences, or effective communications. I believe empathy, inclusivity, and good design can play an instrumental role in delivering better outcomes.


I was previously a fellow with the University of Chicago Harris School of Public Policy’s International Innovation Corps. I introduced user-centered design to a project with the Tata Trusts and Municipal Corporation of Greater Mumbai to improve sanitation and waste management in the city.


I hold a Bachelor’s degree in political science from the University of Chicago, where I took many excellent courses in policy, law, anthropology, and statistics.


Fun fact: This year I did my first weeklong Himalayan trek. It was incredible, and I wrote a photo essay on it. Feedback is welcome!
