Fundraising for Amir Shah, a boy from a Mumbai slum, to attend London’s Royal Academy of Ballet

crowdfunding

video production

Amir was in my ballet class, and when he got accepted into the American Ballet Theatre school in New York, he needed funds to pay for tuition and living costs. He comes from a very low-income background, but he has an incredible natural gift for ballet. I shot and produced a video, and created a crowdfunding campaign on GoFundMe that raised over $40,000 in just a couple months both on and off the platform. Amir was featured in news outlets internationally, including BBC and The Independent. He ended up at London’s Royal Academy of Ballet on a full 3-year scholarship, where he will train to become one of the first male professional ballet dancers to represent India.
