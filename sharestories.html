Going behind the scenes of a full-length ballet production

video production

storytelling

During my time as Executive Director of University Ballet of Chicago, one of the University of Chicago's largest dance groups, I created a series of 3 videos highlighting different people involved in our production of Le Corsaire. The videos tell the story of our amazing costumes mistress, as well as the impressive journey of two male dancers who began ballet in college, and found themselves playing lead roles in our production. Le Corsaire sold out a 700-seat auditorium on its closing night.
