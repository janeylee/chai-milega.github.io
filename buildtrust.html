Redesigning a donation platform

user research

product strategy

visual design

I've always found it fascinating how people choose to spend their money, so when an online donation platform based in Mumbai approached our studio to brainstorm a strategy for increasing retail donations, I was thrilled.

This case study begins with our findings from a month-long user research process with donors in the Indian context. Though the project with the client stopped here, there were many useful findings that could improve specific product features. I took these findings and translated them into redesigns for one of the platform's main pages.
