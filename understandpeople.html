As a fellow with University of Chicago's International Innovation Corps, I had the opportunity to work with an Indian philanthropic foundation and the city of Mumbai to improve the working conditions of urban sanitation workers. This was a fascinating exercise in conducting design research with people from backgrounds drastically different from mine. Our team went through various iterations of our research process, and ended up encouraging individuals within the foundation and city to take a more human-centered approach in making policy interventions.
